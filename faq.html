Questions about joining, assessments, and finding opportunities

Yes, TalentFirst is completely free for candidates. You can:

  • Create a detailed profile showcasing your skills
  • Take up to 5 skill assessments for free
  • Apply to unlimited job opportunities
  • Receive job matches and interview requests
  • Access basic career insights and analytics

We offer premium features like unlimited assessments, priority matching, and advanced analytics for $19/month, but the core platform remains free.

Our skill assessments are designed to verify your real-world abilities:

Our goal is to help you identify skill gaps and provide resources to improve, not to exclude candidates.

Billing & Accounts

Questions about payments, subscriptions, and account management

Cancelling your subscription is simple and can be done at any time:

1

Go to Account Settings

Navigate to your account settings from the dashboard

2

Select Billing

Click on the "Billing & Subscription" tab

3

Cancel Subscription

Click "Cancel Subscription" and confirm your choice

Refund Policy

  • 14-day money-back guarantee: Full refund if cancelled within 14 days of purchase
  • Pro-rated refunds: For annual plans, refunds are pro-rated for unused months
  • No questions asked: We process refunds quickly with minimal verification

Note: After cancellation, you'll retain access to premium features until the end of your billing period.

We accept all major payment methods with enterprise-grade security:

Credit & Debit Cards

Processed securely via Stripe with PCI DSS Level 1 compliance

Digital Wallets

Fast, secure payments without sharing card details

Bank Transfers & Invoicing

Available for enterprise customers with net-30 terms

Security Features

  • End-to-end encryption for all transactions
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II certified
  • Never store raw payment data on our servers

You have full control over your data and account deletion:

Account Deletion Process

  1. Go to Account Settings → Privacy & Data
  2. Click "Request Account Deletion"
  3. Confirm via email for security verification
  4. Receive confirmation of deletion

Data Handling After Deletion

Data Type Retention Period Notes
Personal Information 30 days Permanently deleted from all systems
Assessment Results 90 days Anonymized for platform improvement
Application History 30 days Companies you applied to retain records per their policies

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Download any data you want to keep before proceeding.
